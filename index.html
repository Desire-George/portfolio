Who I Am

I am a dedicated cybersecurity professional and Blue Teamer actively seeking a Junior SOC Analyst role. With a strong foundation in threat intelligence, network monitoring, and incident triage, my goal is to proactively identify, analyze, and remediate security threats to protect organizational infrastructure.

Rather than just learning theory, I take a highly practical approach to cybersecurity. From deploying MISP instances to map Advanced Persistent Threat (APT) infrastructure, to utilizing Ansible Infrastructure as Code (IaC) to automate vulnerability patching via Nessus, I thrive on building resilient, automated defenses.

I am eager to bring my analytical mindset, hands-on lab experience, and passion for continuous improvement to a dynamic Security Operations Center.
